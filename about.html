About Us

Mrs. Simisola Adedeji I was born in Ibadan, a city in Nigeria. Had my early education in Ibadan. A registered general nurse both in Nigeria and United Kingdom. Moved to United Kingdom with my late husband Doctor Edmond Adefolu Adedeji and our three children in 1991. A registered nurse in United Kingdom, with bachelor's degree in health services management and master's degree in public health. My role since 2020 involves Vaccinating school children with various vaccines. I educate parents, guidance, and careers of pupils on the importance and advantages of vaccines to the little ones in primary schools and the young adults in secondary schools. Enjoy working in the community. My skills include computing, effective communication with both young and adults.
